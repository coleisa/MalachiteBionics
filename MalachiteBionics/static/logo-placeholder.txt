LOGO PLACEHOLDER INSTRUCTIONS:

To add your logo:
1. Save your logo image as "logo.png" in this /static folder
2. Recommended size: 64x64 pixels or 128x128 pixels
3. Format: PNG with transparent background preferred
4. The logo will automatically appear in the navbar and footer

Current logo references in templates:
- Navbar: height: 32px
- Footer: height: 24px

The logo image will be automatically resized to fit these dimensions while maintaining aspect ratio.
